About StudyNest

StudyNest by IST is a dedicated digital platform created to serve the academic community of the Institute of Space and Technology (IST), Islamabad.

We provide a simple, organized, and reliable space where students and faculty can access lecture notes, handouts, slides, and other course materials shared directly from departments.

Our goal is to make studying easier by eliminating the hassle of searching for notes across different sources. Everything you need, all in one place, anytime, anywhere.

Key Features

  • Browse notes by department and course
  • Clean and easy-to-use interface
  • Request missing notes with one click
  • Mobile-friendly design for studying on the go
  • Regularly updated with fresh content
